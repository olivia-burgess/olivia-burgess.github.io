A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme

How I will manage myself to work productively and safely with other learners, facilitators, and industry/community representatives:

Maintaining clear communication with teammates and facilitators, while being considerate of each others learning journey. Fostering collaboration among teammates, acknowledging both their strengths and areas where they may need support.

What I will do when things are building up and starting to get to me:

I am fortunate to have the support of my boyfriend, who is a software developer. During the HTML and CSS week, it was challenging and I was struggling a lot, feeling frustrated and doubting my work. He reassured me that my efforts weren't as bad as I thought, encouraging me to keep practicing and refining my skills. Additionally, it's beneficial to connect with others in the course during stressful times like this. Sharing experiences and learning together can be really helpful. When things become overwhelming, I find it most effective to confront them directly. Breaking down tasks into smaller, manageable steps helps me stay focused and organized in completing what I need to do.
